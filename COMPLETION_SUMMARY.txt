================================================================================
                        KEY DIFFERENTIATORS
================================================================================

VS. Traditional Gamification:
  • We verify behaviors (not free coins)
  • We reward discipline (on-time payments, credit improvement)
  • We create meaningful goals (tiers require both coins AND behaviors)
  • We prevent inflation (5 spending sinks)

VS. CRED:
  • We measure financial discipline (not just bill payments)
  • We build credit profiles (future lending product)
  • We verify multiple behaviors (bank, credit bureau, goals)
  • We create ecosystem (commerce + games + social)
